About Our Daycare

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bakwena Daycare has been providing exceptional care and education for children ages 6 weeks to 5 years. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where children can learn, grow, and thrive.

Our Philosophy

We believe in learning through play, exploration, and social interaction. Our curriculum is designed to develop the whole child - emotionally, socially, physically, and intellectually.

Our Team

Our dedicated staff includes certified early childhood educators, nutritionists, and childcare specialists who are passionate about child development.
