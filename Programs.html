Our Programs

Infant Care (6 weeks - 18 months)

Individualized care with focus on sensory development and motor skills

  • 1:4 caregiver to child ratio
  • Daily tummy time and movement
  • Music and sensory activities

Toddler Program (18 months - 3 years)

Structured play and early learning activities

  • Language development
  • Potty training support
  • Social skills development

Preschool (3 - 5 years)

School readiness program

  • Early literacy and math
  • STEM activities
  • Creative arts
