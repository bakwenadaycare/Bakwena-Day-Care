Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 6:30 AM to 6:30 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM for special programs.

Do you offer part-time schedules?

Yes! We offer flexible part-time schedules including 2, 3, or 4 days per week options to meet your family's needs.

What is your staff-to-child ratio?

We maintain ratios better than state requirements: 1:4 for infants, 1:6 for toddlers, and 1:10 for preschoolers.

How can I schedule a tour?

You can schedule a tour by filling out our contact form, calling us directly, or using our online booking system.
